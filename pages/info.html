Here is some information on how to read a product's Nutrition Facts label.

1)Serving Size

The first line is of the label is the Serving Size, which indicates the size of the serving for all the subsequant values that follow. So, for example, on the label to the right for every 1 cup of Macaroni and Cheese, the Protein would be 3g. As you can see the Calories woule be 250, and the Total Fat would be 12g, and so on and so forth.

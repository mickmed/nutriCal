Storing

With personal accounts coming soon, you'll be able to save all your favorite meal plans and recipes. Not to mention tally up to 8 meals per day, so you'll know exactly how much of each nutrient your getting on a daily basis. Oh, did we mention weekly meal planner. Welcome to NutriCal© !

Scanning

Soon you'll be able to scan you're favorite foods right into Nutrical©. You'll instantly access all of the Nutrition Data on the app. Go ahead and portion it for meal planning and recipes. As usual, your values will update automatically!

API sourcing

With future sourcing coming from USDA food composition databases, you'll be able to instantly search for literally tens of thousands of foods. Compare them side by side, compare brand names of similar foods. Search foods by category, or search by nutrient. Just some of the upcoming fun on NutriCal© !
